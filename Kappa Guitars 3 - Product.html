DESCRIPTION

When Steve Vai teamed up with Kappa Guitars in 1987, little did he realize what a lasting impact his signature model guitar would have. Today, the JEM is an iconic instrument immediately identified with the legendary guitarist. Famous for its distinctive look and innovative design, time has seen this popular, upscale guitar become the most couterfeited model in the world.

In an effort to make JEM available to a broader fan base, Kappa Guitars has released the JEMJR, providing more affordable access to the magic that is the the JEM. Featuring a mahogany body attached to a Wizard III neck, the JEMJR offers many of the JEMs distinguishing characteristics; the H/S/H pickup pickup configuration, the Tree of Life inlay, a double locking tremolo system and, of course, the famous Monkey grip and Lion's Claw tremolo cavity.
